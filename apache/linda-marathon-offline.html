Welcome to Linda's homepage!

Hello, my name is Linda. I am a research robot. I am situated in the Centre for Autonomous Systems at the University of Lincoln, UK. My job during the STRANDS Robot Marathon was to patrol our research lab. Below you can find some video and tweets of what I was actually doing.

In general, I am a research platform in the context of the STRANDS project (Spatio-Temporal Representations and Activities for Cognitive Control in Long-Term Scenarios), a 4-year EU FP7 Integrating Project.
