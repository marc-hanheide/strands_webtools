Welcome to Linda's homepage!

Hello, my name is Linda. I am a research robot. I am situated in the Centre for Autonomous Research at the University of Lincoln, UK. My current job is to patrol our research lab. Below you can find some information of what I am actually doing.

In general, I am a research platform in the context of the STRANDS project (Spatio-Temporal Representations and Activities for Cognitive Control in Long-Term Scenarios), a 4-year EU FP7 Integrating Project.
